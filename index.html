ABOUT US

We are a nimble team of four, dedicated to sustainable growth of wealth using systematic automated trading.

Our team comprises of two creatives. One is an options trader, the other is a value investor. Their role is envisage new strategies. They actively participate in the algo community on the lookout for interesting ideas.

We have one coder on the team. He is an engineer. His work includes writing code for back-testing, data management, execution algorithms and ensuring strategy robustness, with a well define encodable premise.

Lastly we have one data cruncher. He is an engineer and an expert with data analytics. He is responsible for post-processing and slippage management. His output feeds back into testing to refine the strategy.

We believe in simple strategies powered by a long period of dense historic data with defined risk, moderate but sustainable returns, and sound money management, indifferent to index direction. We take extreme care to be vigilant of sensational results, which are usually an outcome of over-fitting over, necessarily, a short period of time. The market has a habit of humbling traders who do not suspect similarly.

We share your intent on success since we use what we build on our own capital. We believe this is an incentive is essential to ensure our offering is superlative.
